# PaperBanana

**AI Diagram Generator for Research Papers**

[PaperBanana](https://paper-banana.net/) helps researchers create publication-ready methodology diagrams, scientific plots, and research workflows for papers, slides, and technical documentation.

## Key Features

- **Instant Methodology Diagrams** - Convert text descriptions into structured architecture diagrams or flowcharts instantly
- **High-Precision Statistical Plots** - Generate accurate charts from raw data with professional color palettes
- **Sketch-to-Digital Transformation** - Upload hand-drawn sketches and get vector-quality illustrations
- **Journal-Standard Aesthetics** - Outputs optimized for top-tier journal publication standards
- **Multi-Agent Architecture** - Five specialized agents (Retriever, Planner, Stylist, Visualizer, Critic) work collaboratively

## Use Cases

- **Machine Learning** - Neural network architectures, training pipelines, model diagrams
- **Biomedicine** - Reaction pathways, experimental setup diagrams
- **Educational Materials** - Lecture slides, textbook infographics
- **Economics & Social Science** - Statistical charts, conceptual frameworks

## Getting Started

1. **Input Your Context** - Paste paper abstract, model description, or upload a sketch
2. **Select Your Style** - Choose from Clean Tech, Classic Journal, or Modern AI styles
3. **Generate & Refine** - AI creates your figure; tweak colors, labels, or layouts
4. **Export** - Download publication-ready figures in your preferred format
